Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji is the most popular street food in India that originated in Mumbai. It is an appetising meal that is made with a blend of different vegetables and spices and is typically served with butter-soaked buns.

Dosa

Dosa is famous for its simple ingredients and savory, slightly bitter flavor. It can be eaten as a snack, breakfast, or anytime you're in the mood for a delicious, savory meal!.

Samosa

The samosa is endlessly adaptable and India introduced its own spices - adding coriander, pepper, caraway seeds, ginger and more. The filling changed, too, with vegetables often replacing meat..
